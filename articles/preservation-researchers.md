# 🔬 Long-Term Data Preservation: Module 3

The final step for researchers focuses on archiving data securely for the next 10+ years, ensuring compliance with institutional and funder requirements.

## 📋 Learning Objectives
* Learn about **File Format Longevity** (converting proprietary formats to open formats like `.csv` or `.pdf/A`).
* Set up permanent digital identifiers (DOIs) for your data.
* Draft the final Data Management Plan (DMP) report.

## 🛠️ Practical Checklist
- [ ] Convert all Excel files (`.xlsx`) into open comma-separated values (`.csv`).
- [ ] Generate a permanent DOI link via your chosen repository.
- [ ] Submit the completed DMP to your institution's research office.
