# 📢 Measuring Training Impact: Module 3

The final step for trainers focuses on evaluating the long-term success of your workshops and gathering feedback to iterate on your teaching materials.

## 📋 Learning Objectives
* Utilize **Kirkpatrick's Levels of Evaluation** for educational impact.
* Automate feedback collection using digital survey tools.
* Analyze post-workshop metrics to prove training ROI to stakeholders.

## 🛠️ Practical Checklist
- [ ] Create a standardized post-workshop feedback survey (using Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, etc.).
- [ ] Add a "Self-Assessment" matrix where learners score their confidence before and after the class.
- [ ] Set a calendar reminder to check in with students 30 days post-training to see if they adopted the skills.
