# 💼 Data Governance for Managers: Module 1

As a manager, your role is to ensure compliance, security, and smooth data workflows across your team. This module covers the strategic implementation of data governance frameworks.

## 📋 Learning Objectives
* Understand **GDPR and Compliance** risks within your team's current workflows.
* Design access control matrices (Who needs access to what?).
* Establish protocols for data breaches and storage cost optimizations.

## 🛠️ Practical Checklist
Complete these operational alignment tasks:
- [ ] Review your team's current cloud storage permissions.
- [ ] Revoke access for offboarded employees or external vendors who no longer need it.
- [ ] Schedule a 15-minute compliance sync with your data protection officer (DPO).
